# S10 — Round 3: Post-Capture Deep Qualification + Case Intake Memo

**Status:** InProgress  
**Sprint:** S10  
**Owner:** @dev  
**Created:** 2026-04-17

---

## Problem

After OTP verification, Band A/B leads currently see the Book a Call CTA immediately. The intake process ends the moment identity is confirmed. The lawyer receives a lead row with a CPI score and a situation summary — useful, but indistinguishable from what a firm secretary could produce on a first call.

The product promise is "Sign Better Cases." The intake must produce an artifact that makes that promise visible before the lawyer ever speaks to the prospect.

## Solution

Add a third qualification round (Round 3) after OTP verification. Round 3 is not more scoring — scoring is done. Round 3 is **case construction**: evidence inventory, parties, jurisdiction, narrative depth, and expectations alignment. It runs before the Book a Call CTA unlocks.

The output is a **Case Intake Memo** — a structured document the lawyer reads before the consultation. It collapses everything the intake learned into the artifact a junior associate would prepare. The memo is the product.

**Core positioning line (use verbatim in demo and marketing):**
> Rounds 1 and 2 decide whether to take the meeting. Round 3 decides how the lawyer walks into the meeting prepared.
