Why this access matters

Authenticated email is the difference between inbox and spam

When we send review request emails, follow-up sequences, and any campaign content from your domain, Gmail and Outlook check three records (SPF, DKIM, DMARC) to confirm the message is really from drglaw.ca and not a spoof. Without those records, modern inboxes route your mail to spam at best, and at worst your domain stays open to impersonation by anyone willing to forge it.

The setup is one-time. Once it lands, everything routed through your domain inherits the trust. Every review request, every nurture sequence, every transactional confirmation. The work itself is plumbing, not creative, but it has to happen before any of the Authority and Capture work goes live.

What this access permits, what it does not

Adriano can
  • Enable DKIM signing on drglaw.ca
  • Configure DMARC reporting
  • Manage mail flow rules
  • Set up email aliases and forwarding
  • Verify domain authentication health
  • Configure shared mailboxes if needed later
Adriano cannot
  • Read your email or anyone else's
  • Reset your password
  • Access OneDrive or SharePoint files
  • See your calendar or contacts
  • Change billing or subscriptions
  • Modify other users' accounts

The role being requested is Exchange Administrator. It is the surgical role for email infrastructure work. It is not Global Administrator, which would give blanket access. The DNS-side records (SPF, DMARC) that live at your domain registrar will be sent to you as a separate list, since registrar-level DNS sits outside Microsoft 365 entirely.

Time-boxed and reversible

The grant is intended for a five-business-day window. Once authentication is live and tested, you remove the role from the same screen where you granted it. Step 4 of this guide shows the exact path. Nothing about this is permanent without your active consent.

What removal does
My Exchange administrator role disappears immediately. The guest user record stays in your directory (so any audit trail is preserved), but I can no longer see or change anything in your tenant. To delete the guest record entirely, go to Users › Guest users and remove the entry there.
If anything looks off
Removal is one-click. Use it any time you feel uncertain about the access, even before the work is finished. I would rather you remove and we troubleshoot than leave the assignment open if you have questions.
What happens next

Five days from grant to authenticated email

Once the role is granted, the work runs in parallel between two surfaces. I configure DKIM signing inside Exchange admin center, you paste a small set of DNS records at your domain registrar (where drglaw.ca is hosted, likely GoDaddy). Each record is a one-line copy-paste with values I supply. By end of week, all three protocols are live and you are protected.

Day 0 · Today

Role assignment complete

You complete Steps 1 to 3 above. I receive the invitation, accept, complete MFA. Exchange administrator role is active on my account.

Days 1 to 2

DKIM keys generated, DNS values sent

I generate DKIM signing keys inside Exchange admin center. I send you a list of three DNS records (one for SPF, two CNAMEs for DKIM, one for DMARC) in a clear copy-paste table.

Days 2 to 4

You add the DNS records

You paste the values at your domain registrar. Five-minute job, on its own page in your registrar dashboard. We can do this together over a fifteen-minute call if it is your first time touching DNS.

Day 5

Verified, then revoked

I run a verification test (mail-tester.com or equivalent) to confirm SPF, DKIM, and DMARC pass. I send you the green-light report. You run Step 4 of this guide and remove my admin role.

When you finish Steps 1 to 3

Send me a one-line WhatsApp confirming the assignment is in place. I will accept the invitation within the hour and start the configuration that day.

A note on data privacy

The Exchange administrator role does not give visibility into mailbox content. If you ever want a complete audit of what an admin can see and do, the Microsoft documentation under "About admin roles in the Microsoft 365 admin center" lists every permission attached to every role, sourced directly from Microsoft. The role being requested here is the most surgical option for the work that needs to happen.
