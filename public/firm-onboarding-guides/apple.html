Setup walkthrough · Apple Business Connect

Add CaseLoad Select as Administrator on your Apple Business Connect listing

About twenty minutes. Apple Business Connect controls how your firm appears in Apple Maps, Siri, Spotlight search, and the Apple ecosystem broadly. iPhone holds roughly 60% market share in Canada, and Apple's search results do not route through Google. Without an Apple listing, your firm is invisible on a meaningful slice of "find a lawyer" searches from iPhone users.

Time~20 minutes
You'll needApple ID + iPhone for verification
Sign-in URLbusinessconnect.apple.com

Why we ask you to do the setup on your Apple ID

Apple does not allow one phone to anchor two business listings. Our Apple IDs are tied to our personal phones, so we cannot create the listing for you. You sign up under your own Apple ID, complete verification on your phone, then invite CaseLoad Select as a Team Member with Administrator access. Ongoing management transfers to us. Same arrangement as the GBP and LinkedIn setups: you stay as the owner of record, we handle the day-to-day.

Before you start

Make sure you have access to the iPhone tied to your Apple ID. Apple verification sends a text or makes a phone call to that number. If you don't have an Apple ID, create one at appleid.apple.com first; takes about five minutes.

Six steps. Have your phone in hand.

1

Sign in at businessconnect.apple.com

Use your existing Apple ID. If you don't have one, click "Create an Apple ID" on the sign-in page. Apple may ask for two-factor authentication; have your phone nearby.

[Screenshot · businessconnect.apple.com sign-in screen]
2

Add your business

After signing in, click Add Location or Get Started. Apple may already have an auto-generated listing from public sources; if so, claim it. Otherwise add a new business.

Fill in the firm details exactly as you have them on your other listings (GBP, LinkedIn, Bing). We'll feed you the canonical NAP if you want to copy and paste:

  • Business name (your full legal entity)
  • Category: Lawyer / Legal Services / Law Firm
  • Address, phone, website, hours
[Screenshot · Add Location form with NAP fields highlighted]
3

Verify the listing via your phone

Apple sends a text or makes a call to the business phone number on the listing. You confirm by entering the code Apple sends, or by answering the call.

Verification is required before you can add a team member or before the listing appears in Apple Maps + Siri + Spotlight.

[Screenshot · Verification code entry screen]
4

Company name → Team → Invite Team Member

Once verified, click your company name in the top navigation. From the dropdown, select Team.

On the Team page, click Invite Team Member.

[Screenshot · Team page with Invite Team Member button]
5

Invite adriano@caseloadselect.ca as Administrator

In the invite form:

  • Email: adriano@caseloadselect.ca
  • Role: Administrator

Administrator role lets us edit the listing, publish posts, respond to inquiries, and manage Apple Maps + Siri + Spotlight presence on your behalf. It does not transfer ownership. You stay as the owner of record; you can revoke our access at any time.

[Screenshot · Invite team member form filled with admin role]
6

Send the invitation

Apple emails the invitation to adriano@caseloadselect.ca. We accept the invite from our side within the business day. The listing typically takes 24 to 72 hours to propagate to Apple Maps, Siri, and Spotlight after we finish populating the details.

From here on, you only touch Apple Business Connect if you want to publish something specific. Routine updates go through us.

After you finish

Mark the status as Done in your onboarding form

Go back to your onboarding form and set the Apple Business Connect status to Done: access granted. If you hit a blocker (Apple ID issue, verification failed, role unavailable, two-factor problem), set it to Blocked and write a short note. We'll respond within the business day.
