Meta Business Manager admin access

Why this access matters

WhatsApp Business lives inside Meta Business Manager

The WhatsApp Business Account, the verified phone number, the message templates Meta has to approve before any outbound send, and the verification documents Meta cross-checks against your business records all live inside the Business Manager. Without an admin role there, no WhatsApp configuration is possible.

The role is added by email, not by tenant membership. Meta has no "guest user" model the way Microsoft does. You add me by email, I receive an invite, I accept with my personal Facebook account, and the role activates. You stay Admin alongside me and can remove me from the same screen at any time.

What this access permits, what it does not

CaseLoad Select can
  • Submit WhatsApp message templates to Meta for approval
  • Register and verify the WhatsApp phone number
  • Manage WhatsApp Business Account quality scores and rate limits
  • Upload and manage business verification documents
  • Add and remove WhatsApp business assets we configure together
  • See your business profile and connected business pages
CaseLoad Select cannot
  • Read your personal Facebook profile, posts, or messages
  • Access your personal friends list or photos
  • Post on your personal timeline
  • Modify billing on any ad account unless explicitly granted
  • See other businesses you manage in your Business Manager
  • Take any action outside the assets we are granted

The role being requested is Admin on the firm Business Manager. Admin is the minimum role that can submit WhatsApp templates and complete verifications. Lower roles (Employee) cannot publish templates, which would block the J-workflows from sending.

Reversible at any time

Unlike Microsoft, where the access is time-boxed because the work is one-time, the Meta admin role is ongoing because we keep submitting templates and handling re-verifications as the platform requires them. That said, the role is one-click removable. Step 4 of this guide shows the exact path. You stay in control.

01
Step 1 · About 2 minutes

Sign in at business.facebook.com

Open business.facebook.com in your browser and sign in with your personal Facebook account. Meta does not use business-domain accounts the way Microsoft does. If you do not have a Facebook account, you will need to create one (private, no public profile required) before continuing. The Business Manager home screen loads showing your current business or an offer to create one.

Select the WhatsApp Business Account (and Pages or Ad accounts if you want me to help with those later; for the WhatsApp build, WhatsApp account is the only required asset). 04
Step 4 · About 1 minute · Run only if you want to remove access

Remove me from the Business at any time

Meta admin access is ongoing because we keep submitting templates and handling re-verifications. There is no time-boxed window the way there is on Microsoft. That said, removal is one click. Back in Business Settings → Users → People, find my row, click the menu icon at the right end, and choose Remove from business. The role disappears immediately and I lose access to every asset in the business.

What removal does
My role on every business asset disappears instantly. The person record stays in your directory (so the audit trail of who-had-what-and-when is preserved), but the access permissions are revoked. Any WhatsApp templates already approved by Meta stay approved; my ability to submit new ones or modify existing ones ends at the same moment.
If anything looks off
Removal is one-click and immediate. Use it any time you feel uncertain about the access, even mid-build. I would rather you remove the role and we troubleshoot than leave it open if you have questions. We can always re-add it the same day.
What happens next

From admin grant to first WhatsApp send

Once the role is granted, the WhatsApp setup runs through a sequence Meta dictates: register the phone number, verify the business with a document, get the display name approved, submit message templates one by one. Each step has a Meta-side approval window of one to three days. The path below is the typical one for a Toronto law firm in 2026.

Day 0 · Today

Admin role accepted

You complete Steps 1 to 3. I receive the Meta invitation email, accept it, and the role is live on my account.

Days 1 to 3

WhatsApp number registered

I add the GHL phone number to your WhatsApp Business Account, complete the SMS or voice verification, and set up the display name (DRG Law) for Meta approval.

Days 3 to 5

Business verification

I upload the verification document you provided in the onboarding form. Meta cross-checks it against the Business Manager profile and approves the business — usually within 48 hours.

Days 5 to 7

Templates approved, first send live

Message templates for the J-workflows (acknowledgment, no-show recovery, review request) get submitted and approved one by one. Once the first template clears, WhatsApp goes live for outbound sends from your number.

When you finish Steps 1 to 3

Send me a quick WhatsApp confirming the invitation is in your queue or already accepted on my end. I will accept within the hour and start the WhatsApp setup that day.

A note on data privacy

Meta admin access on a Business Manager does not give visibility into your personal Facebook activity. The Business Manager is a separate context from your personal account; the same login authenticates both, but the permissions are scoped to business assets only. If you ever want a full audit of what an admin can see and do, Meta's documentation under People in your business lists every permission attached to every role.
