Put your client history to work

One list unlocks three systems that are already built and waiting: conflict protection on every new lead, a review engine for the firm's Google profile, and a reactivation cadence for the clients who already trust you. This sheet covers exactly what to send and how it stays protected.

01

Conflict protection

Every new lead is checked against your client history before a consultation is booked. Internal use only; nobody on the list is contacted for this.

02

Review engine

Recent clients receive an invitation to review the firm on Google. You approve the wording before anything goes out. Reviews carry the local search profile.

03

Repeat and referral

Leases renew, companies grow, wills age. A quiet cadence keeps the firm present when the next matter surfaces, and makes referring you easy.

How it stays protected

You own the data

Pilot Agreement, Section 7: DRG Law is the sole owner. CaseLoad Select processes it only on the firm's behalf, under PIPEDA.

Canadian servers

Lead and intake records are stored in Montreal. Access is limited to the operator running your system.

CASL by default

Contacts past the two-year consent window receive no email or SMS. They stay in the conflict register only.

Exit terms in writing

Full export within 30 days of termination. Permanent deletion within 15 days after. Already signed, already binding.
